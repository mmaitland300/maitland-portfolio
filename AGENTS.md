## Git and release

- Use a **new branch** from current `main`; do not commit on `main` for routine work.
- **Do not `git push` to any remote** unless the maintainer explicitly asked you to publish. Leave commits local until they review and push (or instruct you to push a named branch).
- **Never push `origin/main`** without explicit maintainer approval after review. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
